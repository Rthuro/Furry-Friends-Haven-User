ABOUT US



Dedicated to rescuing and rehoming animals in need, our mission is to provide a loving forever home for every furry friend that crosses our path. The heartbreak of abandonment and the joy of finding a forever home, fuels our passion to create a brighter future for animals in need.


Change a life today, one paw at a time.


Our shelter is located at Canelar, Zamboanga City.


Furry Friends' Haven relies on the community's generosity to provide food, shelter, and medical care for our homeless pets. Through donations or by opening your heart and home to a deserving animal, you're not just giving them a second chance, but are making a world of difference in the lives of these animals.

ADOPTION PROCESS

We make adopting a pet easy! Browse for a pet, fill out our online application, schedule a brief interview, and then meet your potential match. We're here to guide you through every step.



Important Things to Consider Before Adopting:


  • Our adoption fee covers everything your new furry friend needs to be healthy and happy before coming home with you (vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and more!).
  • Adopting a pet is a lifelong commitment. Are you prepared to provide love, care, and training for their entire life?
